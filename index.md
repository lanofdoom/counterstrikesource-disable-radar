## Installation
Extract ``lan_of_doom_disable_radar.tar.gz`` to your server's ``css/cstrike``
directory.

## Console Variables
``sm_lanofdoom_radar_disabled`` If ``1``, radar is disabled for all players.
``1`` by default.
